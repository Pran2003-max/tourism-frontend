Popular Destinations

Netarhat

Known as the 'Queen of Chotanagpur', Netarhat offers breathtaking sunrise and sunset views from its hilltop location.

Sunrise Point Sunset Point Magnolia Point

Patratu

Famous for the Patratu Valley and Dam, offering stunning landscapes and boating opportunities.

Patratu Dam Valley Views Boating

Betla National Park

Home to tigers, elephants, and diverse wildlife in the heart of Palamu district.

Wildlife Safari Tiger Reserve Nature Trails

Hundru Falls

A spectacular 98-meter waterfall on the Subarnarekha River, perfect for nature lovers.

98m Waterfall Swimming Photography

Deoghar

Sacred city known for the Baidyanath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas.

Baidyanath Temple Nandan Pahar Tapovan

Local Culture & Heritage

Festivals

Sarhul

Spring festival celebrating nature and new beginnings

Karma Puja

Harvest festival with traditional dance and music

Karam

Celebration of the Karam tree with folk performances

Tribal Art

Paitkar Paintings

Traditional scroll paintings depicting mythological stories

Tribal Crafts

Handwoven baskets, pottery, and wood carvings

Folk Music

Traditional songs and instruments like Mandar and Nagara

Pittha
